What is Local Hack Day?

Local Hack Day is a worldwide event organized by Major League Hacking and hosted by GitHub. Think of it as a mini-hackathon. The event brings together students from across the world for one day of hacking. To learn more and see other school participating in Local Hack Day, see localhackday.mlh.io.
